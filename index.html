About

Ron Newton is a sole practitioner dedicated to providing dynamic and personal attention to the needs of his clients.

With over 30 years of hands on experience in the area of contracts and negotiations, Ron can provide the client a unique and practical application to the formation, review and implementation of contracts as well to the successful completion of negotiations whether contract related or otherwise.

As president of his local school board, Ron continues to be deeply committed to area of educational law and how it applies to the student.

Ron earned his Bachelor of Arts degree from Tulane University and his Juris Doctorate from Western State University School of Law. His California Bar Number is 315530. He is a member of the San Bernardino County Bar Association, the Riverside County Bar Association, the Orange County Bar Association and the American Bar Association.
